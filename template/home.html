AI Health Center

Welcome to the home page of AI Health Center.

This is mainly based on a application of the machine learning, meant to be deployed in the remote and the downtrodden area to give free medical diagnosis.

Overview

In today's time we see a lot of the shortage of the doctors in the world especially in Philippines. A lot of people are suffering a lot without the help of the proper medical checkup.

Also most of the cases many cases arise leading to dealth due to lack of timely medical checkup, so to cope up with all of those problems this app is designed which would prove its benefits up to much extent.

Application

  • Remove the dependencies on the doctors
  • Help out the poor and helpless people with the normal medical checkup
  • Help people avoid paying huge amount to the doctors unnecessarily
  • Extend the role of the technology in the medical field
